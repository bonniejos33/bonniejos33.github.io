I've always loved baking!

If you know me, you may have received one of my famous rum cakes for the holidays. A few years ago, I took a break from baking in order to eat healthier. I finally asked myself, “Why isn’t there a healthier dessert choice that actually tastes good?”. That’s when I came up with my macaroon recipe: Dairy free, gluten free and all natural, using organic products whenever possible. I’m so excited to be baking again and to share my desserts with you!

Thanks to all of you,

Bonnie Jo
